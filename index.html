About Us

Founded in 2010, Chevy Chase began with a vision to revolutionize bathroom design through innovative glass solutions. Our journey started with a small team of skilled craftsmen dedicated to creating custom shower enclosures that would transform ordinary bathrooms into extraordinary spaces. Over the years, we've grown to become a leading provider of premium glass solutions, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the region.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional quality and design innovation in every project we undertake. We believe in the power of thoughtful design to enhance daily living spaces, and we're committed to using only the finest materials and most advanced technologies. Our core values of craftsmanship, integrity, and customer satisfaction guide everything we do, from initial consultation to final installation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation typically take?

Most standard installations are completed within 1-2 days. Custom projects may require additional time depending on complexity and special requirements.

What type of warranty do you offer?

We provide a comprehensive 10-year warranty on glass and hardware, covering manufacturing defects and installation workmanship.

How do I maintain my shower glass?

Our hydrophobic coating significantly reduces maintenance needs. Regular cleaning with a squeegee after use and occasional mild cleaner application is all that's required.

What is the cost range for your services?

Pricing varies based on size, design complexity, and chosen features. We offer free consultations to provide accurate quotes tailored to your specific needs.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with several financial institutions to offer flexible payment plans and financing options to suit various budgets.
